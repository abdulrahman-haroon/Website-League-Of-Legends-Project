MORE ABOUT LEAGUE

GET TO KNOW

There’s a lot to learn about League, so we’ll start with the essentials. League of Legends is a team-based strategy game where two teams of five powerful champions face off to destroy the other’s base. Choose from over 140 champions to make epic plays, secure kills, and take down towers as you battle your way to victory.

Destroy the base. The Nexus is the heart of both teams’ bases. Destroy the enemy’s Nexus first to win the game.
